Welcome to
VAIRAMS group of institutions

VAIRAMS group of institutions is already successfully running a Matriculation School in Pudukkottai for the last three decades. The VAIRAMS PUBLIC SCHOOL is spread over 5 acres of land with two blocks. The school is located in perumanadu, Just 5 kilometers away from the town limit. The primary section works out in the south block while the north block houses classrooms for the middle and high school.

About Us

DESCRIPTION

Our school is located in lush green sprawling campus of 5 acres. We cater more to the needs of the rural folk. Excellent infrastructure with all modern amenities and dedicated teaching fraternity is a feather on our cap.

OUR VISION

We aim at grooming the kids to be responsible citizens and practice core values inculcated at school. Emphasize on holistic education and Good Leadership Qualities.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide safe learning atmosphere in a serene environment inspiring the students to scale the height of success through Education.
