Welcome to VAIRAMS group of institutions

VAIRAMS Group of Institutions has been successfully running a Matriculation School in Pudukkottai for the last three decades. VAIRAMS PUBLIC SCHOOL is spread over of 5 acres of land with two blocks. The school is located in Perumanadu, Just 5 kilometers away from the town limit. The primary section works out of the south block, while the north block houses classrooms for the middle and high school. All classrooms are digitally enabled, with Panel boards and projectors, to enhance the teaching- learning.

There are spacious and well ventilated classrooms, smart class, well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Computer laboratories , well stocked libraries , Audio Visual Rooms , Exam halls , Sports grounds for Football, Basketball, Badminton , Volleyball , provision for indoor activities such as Table tennis , Chess carom. It also has an Open Air Auditorium, Yoga Hall , Kindergarten Activity room and so on. The staff and management of the school firmly believe in creating a platform for children to develop critical thinking and problem-solving capabilities while also molding them into responsible adults
